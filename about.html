About Us

Building trust through quality delivery, safety excellence, and long-standing industry experience.

Company Overview

EDSAN Global Resources is a 100% indigenous Nigerian company with decades of experience in oilfield construction, engineering services and quality inspection. Originally founded in 1994 and formally incorporated in 1999, the company operates under a proven business model focused on quality, safety and client satisfaction.

Our work is never considered complete without the final approval of our clients. Every project is subjected to stringent quality control measures, detailed inspections and performance testing before handover.

Our Vision

To set new industry benchmarks by delivering exceptional inspection and maintenance services, along with competitive solutions that consistently surpass client expectations in the oil and gas sector.

Our Mission

Our mission at Edsan Global is to ensure safe, efficient, and reliable operations in the oil and gas industry by providing world-class inspection and maintenance services. We are committed to delivering value-driven solutions, fostering long-term partnerships, and upholding the highest standards of safety, integrity, and innovation.

Experience Built on the Field

Delivering engineering, inspection, and oilfield construction services with proven expertise, safety discipline, and quality assurance.

Community Affairs Policy

As a company operating across various host communities, we integrate community affairs into our HSE operations. Our approach ensures mutual understanding, peaceful coexistence and sustainable development.

  • Early engagement with host community representatives
  • Establishment of Community Affairs Committees
  • Regular community meetings to resolve issues amicably
  • Dedicated liaison officers on project sites
  • Promotion of peaceful coexistence through incentive programs
