Our Partners

Strategic partnerships that strengthen our capabilities and ensure consistent value delivery across all projects.

EDSAN Global Resources leverages strategic partnerships with leading local and international organizations to enhance service delivery, technical capacity, and operational excellence.

Through these collaborations, we deliver integrated solutions that meet client requirements while maintaining high standards of quality, safety, and efficiency.

Partner Logo

AISTEIN S.L — Valencia, Spain

AISTEIN S.L specializes in industrial insulation systems based on advanced Polyisocyanurate panels used for warehouses, cold rooms, modular housing, and refrigeration facilities.

The company provides customized designs and delivers energy-efficient modular housing solutions engineered for demanding environments requiring precise thermal control.

NUEVA TERRAIN S.L — Bilbao, Spain

NUEVA TERRAIN S.L manufactures and supplies advanced hot and cold water piping systems using polybutylene (PB) and PVC sound-proof drainage systems for soil and wastewater management.

Their solutions support professional plumbing and heating installations, including Push-Fit systems, electrofusion, socket fusion, butt welding systems, and a wide range of accessories.

Amusement Logic — Valencia, Spain

Amusement Logic is a global leader in the design, planning, development, and construction of leisure and tourism complexes, including turnkey projects.

With a multidisciplinary team of engineers, architects, consultants, and creatives, the company delivers complete solutions for hotels, sports facilities, themed attractions, amusement parks, and tourism developments of all sizes and complexities.

Environmental Remediation Partnership

In collaboration with Institute Remediation Service Limited, EDSAN Global Resources has designed, implemented, and maintained soil and groundwater remediation programs across diverse geological environments.

Services include in-situ remediation, ex-situ remediation, and comprehensive remediation support for petroleum hydrocarbon- impacted sites.
