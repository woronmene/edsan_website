Welcome

Edsan Global Limited is a 100% indigenous Nigerian company, with a strong reputation for delivering reliable engineering and maintenance solutions. Since its establishment in 2007 and incorporation in 2017, Edsan has consistently provided end-to-end technical support, ensuring operational efficiency, safety, and compliance for clients across government, public, and private sectors.

Our engineering and maintenance services cover facility management, equipment inspection, preventive maintenance, and technical upgrades, all executed under stringent quality control measures. With a client-focused approach, we guarantee that every project is completed to the highest standards and only handed over after rigorous testing and final client approval.
