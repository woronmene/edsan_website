Health, Safety & Environment

Our commitment to safety, environmental protection, and the wellbeing of our workforce and host communities.

Our HSE Commitment

The HSE Management System of EDSAN Global Resources is committed to the promotion of health, safety, and environmental protection across all operations. We subscribe to the philosophy that all accidents are preventable, and that safety behaviour must be embedded as a way of life in achieving our goal of zero incidents.

Through effective planning, implementation, monitoring, and review, HSE has been fully integrated as a core business tool in the realization of our organizational objectives. Management demonstrates strong commitment through the provision of adequate resources, resulting in reduced incidents, environmentally responsible operations, and improved occupational health services.

Safety First. Always.

Building a safety-driven culture across all operations, personnel, and host communities.

Our HSE Policy

EDSAN Global Resources operates an integrated HSE Policy applicable to all employees and contractors. All personnel are required to comply with company policies and standards, while contractors must maintain policies aligned with ours. Where necessary, support is provided to ensure full compliance.

  • HSE policy orientation for all new employees
  • Confirmation of understanding by the Safety Officer
  • Clear responsibility for personal safety on and off duty
  • Policy displayed on notice boards and communicated in local languages
  • Second and third-tier HSE policies addressing specific operational risks

Health, Safety & Environment Principles

  • Continuously pursue the goal of no harm to people and the environment
  • Manage HSE matters as critical business activities
  • Promote a shared safety culture across all employees and contractors
  • Identify hazards, risks, and sensitivities systematically
  • Implement effective control measures and emergency responses
  • Continuously assess and reduce environmental impact
  • Include HSE performance in staff appraisal and recognition

Weekly safety lectures and continuous training programs reinforce safety awareness and ensure strict adherence to HSE standards.
