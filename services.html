Our Services

Delivering a broad spectrum of engineering, inspection, and technical services to multinational, industrial, and private sector clients.

Over the years, EDSAN Global Resources has been actively involved in providing comprehensive engineering and related services to multinational oil and gas companies, industrial groups, and private clients. Our services are structured into specialized service groups, ensuring efficiency, quality delivery, and strict compliance with industry standards.

Operational Capabilities

Inspection & Engineering Operations

Certified inspection, testing, and engineering services delivered with safety, accuracy, and operational discipline.

Service Group I – Inspection Services

  • Quality Assurance & Quality Control (QA/QC) Services
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) including: Radiographic Testing (RT), Ultrasonic Testing (UT), Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI), Dye Penetrant Inspection (DPI)
  • Welding Inspection Services
  • Post Weld Heat Treatment (PWHT) Services

Other Service Groups

Service Group II – Supply & Support

Provision of technical supplies, logistics support, and operational assistance for project execution.

Service Group III – Environmental & Remediation

Environmental management, remediation services, and compliance support for sustainable operations.

Service Group IV – General Engineering

Mechanical, civil, and electrical engineering services for oilfield and industrial projects.

Service Group V – Manufacturer Representation

Acting as authorized representatives for equipment and technology manufacturers.

Service Group VI – Software Development & Digital Solutions

Development of software applications, digital platforms, and technology-driven solutions supporting operational efficiency.
